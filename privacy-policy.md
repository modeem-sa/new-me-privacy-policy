# Privacy Policy — New Me

**Last updated:** April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **New Me** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Full name, email address, and phone number when you register.
- **Delivery addresses:** Street address, city, and geographic coordinates you save for delivery purposes.
- **Profile photo:** An image you voluntarily upload to personalize your account.
- **Support and inquiry messages:** Text you submit through the in-app contact and inquiry forms.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage data:** Screens visited, features used, and interactions within the App — collected to improve performance and user experience.
- **Push notification tokens:** A Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token is generated automatically to deliver order status updates and promotional notifications.
- **Crash reports:** Anonymous crash and error logs collected via Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix technical issues.

### 1.3 Location Information
We request access to your device's location **only when you are selecting or confirming a delivery address**. Location data is used solely for that purpose and is not tracked in the background.

## 3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed through a **third-party payment gateway** accessed via a secure in-app browser (WebView). We do **not** receive, process, or store your credit or debit card details. All payment data is handled directly by the payment provider under their own privacy and security standards.

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service providers:** Third-party vendors that help operate the App (e.g., Firebase by Google for notifications and analytics). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only to perform services on our behalf.
- **Legal obligations:** When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
- **Business transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
